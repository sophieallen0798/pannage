What We're About

Pannage Pork is a small operation in Oxford, North Carolina. We raise Berkshire-Yorkshire cross pigs with room to roam and real food.

Plenty of Space

Our pigs have room to root, run, and do pig things.

Real Food

The pigs eat well—including produce from local farmers markets that would otherwise go to waste. They root and forage in pasture and forest. Good for them, good for the community.
